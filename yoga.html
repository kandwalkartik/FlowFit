    Yoga Workouts

    Explore our yoga exercises for flexibility, mindfulness, and well-being.

    Sun Salutation

    Steps:

    1. Start in Mountain Pose
    2. Raise arms into Upward Salute
    3. Forward Bend
    4. Plank to Chaturanga
    5. Upward Dog to Downward Dog
    6. Return to Mountain Pose

    Downward Dog

    Steps:

    1. Start in Tabletop Pose
    2. Lift hips up and back
    3. Straighten legs to form V shape
    4. Hold and breathe for 1-3 minutes

    Child's Pose

    Steps:

    1. Start in Tabletop Pose
    2. Sit back onto heels
    3. Stretch arms forward
    4. Hold and breathe for 1-3 minutes
